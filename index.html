The William & Mary Robotics Club is a collaborative, project based space focused on building solutions to meet the needs of our community. We are open to people of any skill level and of any discipline! Our club aims to facilitate a welcoming space for interdisciplinary engineering outside of classes at W&M. We have regular meetings eaturing workshops and activities, while dedicated project teams meet weekly to work on ongoing initiatives, like the Westinghouse robot project with Robin James.
Additionally, from panels at events like TechFusion and &hacks to speakers at general body meetings, robotics club has and continues to welcome guest speakers from industry and academia. If you're interested in speaking, collaborating, or getting involved, please let us know!

Project Teams

Our project team meets weekly to work on continued projects. We also help with projects outside the club, such as the Westinghouse robot project with Robin James.
